# Species Atlas

The Species Atlas is the biological foundation of Mori Mountain.

It organizes all plant systems into structured, comparable, and research-ready units.

---

## Purpose

This section documents all plant species and cultivars studied or cultivated within Mori Mountain systems.

Each species is treated as a **living data model**, integrating:
- biology
- genetics
- ecology
- cultivation systems
- market relevance
- experimental data

---

## Structure

Each species system contains:

- Species overview page
- Cultivar or subspecies pages
- Experimental records
- Comparative analysis pages
