# 🌱 Plant Library

Welcome to the Mori Mountain Plant Library. This is the living catalog of our cold-climate genetics, active seedling nursery, and future cultivar wishlist. 

As we document our agricultural systems, each established species will eventually get its own dedicated research page with soil biology requirements, cold tolerance thresholds, and field performance logs.
