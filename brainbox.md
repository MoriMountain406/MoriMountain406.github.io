# 💡 Open Innovation Lab

Welcome to the Mori Mountain BrainBox. This is our open-source ideation space and public R&D lab. 

Ideas published here are designed to be shared, tested, and improved by the community. We believe that ecological and agricultural solutions should grow collaboratively.

## ⚠️ The Open License

Unless otherwise stated, all concepts in this section are released into the public domain (CC0-equivalent intent). 

**You are free to:**
* Use these concepts
* Modify them
* Build on them
* Commercialize them

No permission or attribution is required, though we always love hearing how these ideas evolve in the wild. Open collaboration is encouraged.
